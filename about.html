Greetings & Welcome to Bin Masud Trading Company Limited.

Est February 2018

BMTCL pushes the limits of technology to create visual systems of the utmost quality. BMTCL has always been innovative by launching new concepts and services; integrated solutions to customers in the field of General Supplies. Bin Masud Trading Company Limited was formed and committed to create a workplace where the creative mind and the brave heart for challenges can realize their full potential. We believe In a common future where everyone's contribution is vital and the growth of the company is the growth of everyone in the company. We take pride In the Kenya history, great heritage, rich culture, and the resiliency of the Kenyan people. With this spirit, we hope to play a part in building this country for the better future.

  • Our Vision

    • Our Vision is to grow our multidisplinary team in order to offer a broad spectrum of specialized Engineering and General Supply Services to become our clients specialized Professional Service Provider.

  • Our Mission

    • The mission of the company is to provide its clients with wide range of value added, complete, high quality and `state-of-the-art`services based on the Earth Observation technology. BMTCL brings to its domestic and international clients complete portfolio of construction & supplies services.

  • Our Values

    • Customer and employee satisfaction is the key to progression and growth of any company.
    Providing the best quality of work by investing in the latest technological trends.
    Employee satisfaction by providing fringe benefits and attractive salaries in order to boom the efficiency within the company itself.
    Internal strength in order to produce the best work.

Being a responsible company is a natural consequence of the BMTCL approach. We recognize that we do not operate in isolation of the society of which the Company and its employees are part and, as such, have made Corporate Social Responsibility an integral part of our operations and strategy. This approach shapes everything we do so that all our activities contribute to the success of the Company both as a business and an employer.
